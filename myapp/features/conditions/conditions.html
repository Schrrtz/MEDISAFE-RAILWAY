Aim for 150 minutes of moderate activity weekly. Exercise strengthens your heart and boosts mental health.

Include fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. A balanced diet provides essential nutrients.

Annual Check-ups

Regular physical exams help detect health issues early when they're most treatable.

  • Blood pressure screening

Stay protected against preventable diseases with recommended vaccines.

  • Tetanus booster (every 10 years)

  • Avoid tobacco products
  • Limit alcohol consumption
  • Manage stress effectively
